Inputs:
    mapguids.py output for historical db
    mapguids.py output for operational db
    zendmd guidtable dump

 Format / content:

 mapguids output:
 <metric_name> <key> <GUID> <count>

zendmd guidtable dump
('GUID', 'path')

Note: To disable intermingling of log messages and data:
 replace logback.xml in /opt/opentsdb/build/src/...
 with this one: https://github.com/oozie/opentsdb/blob/6f0ba2049c2812c1de8f18368b64598c23d1b9e7/src/logback.xml
 (raw: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/oozie/opentsdb/6f0ba2049c2812c1de8f18368b64598c23d1b9e7/src/logback.xml)
 (short raw: https://git.io/vXO4N)
 and redirect stderr to file (or read from stderr)
